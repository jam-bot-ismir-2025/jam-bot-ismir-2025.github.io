The jam_bot, a Real-Time System for Collaborative Free Improvisation with Music Language Models

Additional Resources

This page documents 5 musical examples of Anon Visiting Artist using the jam_bot. Every example demonstrates an interaction strategy through a video or audio recording and an excerpt of the training data used to fine-tune the underlying Music Language Model.
